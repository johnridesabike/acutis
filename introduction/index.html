Why Acutis?

The template engines that inspired Acutis are all great software, and you will be served very well by them. So why use this?

I created Acutis because there were specific language features I wanted. I was inspired by the functional, component-based, composable design in React as well as the pattern-matching abilities in ReScript (and OCaml, Reason, and all of the other languages in that family).
